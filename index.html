Q.E.D.

Fantastic Arcade 2013
Official Selection

QED is a game of curiosity, exploration, playful collaboration, and emergent comedy.

Oh, and dancing sausages.

An experiment in raw kinaesthetics, it pulls inspiration in equal measure from Skate 3, Eric B and Rakim, Noby Noby Boy, and Breakin' 2: Electric Boogaloo.

Development of QED is currently on hold, but some day it may emerge from its sett and gaze with shining eyes upon the glorious, glowing light of the sun.
