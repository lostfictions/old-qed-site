Q.E.D.

Fantastic Arcade 2013
Official Selection

QED is a game of curiosity, exploration, playful collaboration, and emergent comedy.

Oh, and dancing sausages.

(Don't ask. Please. I'm serious.)

An experiment in raw kinaesthetics that pulls inspiration in equal measure from Skate 3, Eric B and Rakim, Noby Noby Boy, and Breakin' 2: Electric Boogaloo.
